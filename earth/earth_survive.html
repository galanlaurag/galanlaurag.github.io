Classic Earth - survive

You stayed in the hospital for a few long years, managed to get through the worst. Your family's support keeps you alive.

Still, the situation on the planet didn't change much. There are new viruses coming up all the time, chances are that you are in higher risk staying in the hospital among all these sick, potentialy contagious people.

Now, that you are able to decide, you can either stay in the hospital (maybe even forever) or leave at your own risk. If you choose leaving, you can either go back home to your family and probably die there because of your unstable condition, or escape to the forest to try healing with herbs on your own.
