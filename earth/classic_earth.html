Classic Earth

It seemed to be the most rational choice as you probably know that earth is pretty safe to live on. However, you were born in difficult times. There is a world's pandemic, most of people living here are either almost dead or already dead. You are extremely weak and sick too. It is nothing you could expect...

You are lucky to be given a choice now - you can run away from this mess here - change the planet and start over, or you can try to survive here.
