Classic Earth - adventure

This is so exciting! You are finally living the life of your dreams. At least until it's starting to get dark...
The sun went down almost completely, you are alone in the wild for the first time in your life. You are terrified.
At the very last time that you were able to see anything in the dark woods, you notice some kind of a teleport - probably to a different world. You have a decision to make - teleport yourself to the even more unknown, or stay in the dangerous, unsafe, wild Earth all alone?
