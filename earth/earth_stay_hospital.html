Classic Earth - hospital

After few more years you finally got better - doctors stabilised your condition enough so you can safely go back home to your family.

After all these years, you finally get to experience what it's like to have a real home. This feels nice at first, you live with people who love you, but this is not something you got use to.

Such a long stay in the hospital made you desire adventures, living life to the fullest when you finally will be able to do so. Now, you have a safe space to stay and live your life, or you can choose to begin a journey to the unknown.
