Classic Earth - hospital

After few more years you finally got better - doctors stabilised your condition enough so you can safely leave hospital and go back home to your family.
After all these years, you finally get to experience what it's like to have a real home. This feels nice at first, you live with people who love you. It definitely isn't life you got use to.

Such a long stay in the hospital made you desire adventures, living life to the fullest when you finally will be able to do so. Now, you have a safe space to stay and live your human life, or you can choose to begin a journey to the unknown.
